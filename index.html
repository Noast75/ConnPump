Linear Axisymmetric Peristaltic Pump

Noah Taub, Bryan Onody, Rudhra Merchant, Leo Zhang, Anthony Rivera-Calva

This design used inflatable tubes to apply axisymmetric pressure along the tube, causing constriction. An air compressor would inflate the tubes using a rotating nozzle which would rotate around the bottom of the track, inflating each tube one after the other, resulting in a peristaltic flow.
