Our Inspiration

When presented with the challenge to design a pump that would not fully occlude, the team knew how much of an impact could be made on the medical field. A pump that does not damage a patients red blood cells would prove to be game changing innovation in the intravenous pump industry. The goal to create this is one the team finds to be a great and meaninful senior design project.
